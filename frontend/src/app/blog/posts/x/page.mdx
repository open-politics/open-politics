
## Vision & Mission

Our vision is a world where everyone has access to comprehensive, trustworthy political information. We believe that informed citizens are the cornerstone of a healthy democracy, and we're committed to providing the tools and insights needed to navigate today's complex political environment.

Our mission is to create novel open-source tools that retrieve, analyze, and present political information in ways that are accessible to everyone. We're combining advanced AI technologies with user-friendly interfaces to break down barriers to political understanding.
